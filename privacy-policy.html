Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Digi Develops ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled safely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or purchase our digital products.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to deliver our services and products to you:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Transaction Data: Details of the digital products you have purchased. (Note: We do not store your credit card or bank account details directly; payments are processed through secure third-party gateways).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • To deliver the digital products you have purchased.
  • To send purchase confirmations and invoices.
  • Communication via WhatsApp: We use your phone number to send order updates, support messages, and relevant promotional offers via WhatsApp. By providing your number, you consent to receive such communications.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. However, we may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners.

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (such as WhatsApp/Meta) to facilitate our communication and customer support. These parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential.

5. Data Security

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your personal information.

6. Your Rights & Opt-Out

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you.

  • Opt-Out: If you no longer wish to receive marketing messages via WhatsApp or Email, you may reply with the word "STOP" or "Unsubscribe" at any time, and we will remove you from our mailing list immediately.

Digi Develops has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. We encourage users to check this page frequently for any changes.
